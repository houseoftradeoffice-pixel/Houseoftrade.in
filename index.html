The Process

How It Works

Three structured steps. No complexity. Your holdings stay intact throughout — only the capital inside them is put to work.

🪙
The Concept
Think of it like a Gold Loan — but for your portfolio

Pledge your MF/ETF holdings as collateral. Unlock margin capital. Deploy it in index options — while your original portfolio keeps growing untouched.

01
You Bring Your Portfolio

₹10L in Mutual Funds, ETFs, or Stock Holdings. They remain in your name and your account at all times. We never hold, transfer, or touch them. They continue earning their natural 8–12% per annum.

₹10L Portfolio — earns 8–12% naturally
02
We Unlock Margin From It

80% of your portfolio value (after the exchange's standard 20% haircut) is released as trading margin. Combined with your ₹10L cash component, this creates ₹18L of active deployable capital.

₹18L Active capital deployed in strategy
03
Index Options Strategy Executes

7+ years of structured index options and derivatives experience is applied on the ₹18L active capital. Returns are generated from both engines simultaneously — without disrupting your holdings.
